Abstract

摘要

The deep integration of data systems and artificial intelligence is reshaping how data is collected, managed, and transformed into knowledge and decisions. Modern AI workloads, ranging from large language models and multimodal learning to domain-specific analytics, are increasingly data-centric and system-driven, relying on scalable data infrastructures, efficient data pipelines, and robust data governance. In data-intensive domains such as healthcare and enterprise intelligence, these challenges are further amplified by data heterogeneity, quality requirements, privacy constraints, and the need for trustworthy and efficient AI.
The workshop Data × AI: Systems, Learning, and Applications provides a focused forum to explore this convergence from a holistic perspective. It brings together research on system foundations, learning paradigms, and real-world applications. By bridging these layers, the workshop aims to highlight emerging challenges, share principled solutions, and identify open research directions that span the full Data × AI stack.
We warmly welcome researchers and practitioners from academia and industry to join this workshop. Through invited talks, technical presentations, and open discussions, the workshop seeks to foster cross-disciplinary collaboration across data management, AI, and application domains, and to advance a shared understanding of how Data × AI co-design can enable robust, efficient, and trustworthy intelligent systems.

数据系统与人工智能的深度融合正在重塑数据的采集、管理以及向知识与决策转化的方式。现代人工智能工作负载涵盖大语言模型、多模态学习以及领域特定分析等，日益呈现出以数据为中心、以系统为驱动的特征，依赖于可扩展的数据基础设施、高效的数据流水线以及健全的数据治理。在医疗健康和商业智能等数据密集型领域中，数据异构性、质量要求、隐私约束以及对可信高效人工智能的需求，使这些挑战进一步加剧。
“Data × AI: 系统、智能与应用”研讨会从整体视角聚焦这一融合趋势，汇聚系统基础、学习范式与实际应用等方面的研究成果。通过贯通这些层面，研讨会旨在揭示新兴挑战，分享有理论支撑的解决思路，并探讨贯穿整个 Data × AI 技术栈的开放性研究方向。
我们诚挚欢迎来自学术界和工业界的研究人员与实践者参加本次研讨会。通过特邀报告、学术论文展示与开放式讨论，研讨会将促进数据管理、人工智能及应用领域之间的交叉合作，推动形成对 Data × AI 协同设计如何支撑稳健、高效、可信智能系统的共同认识。
