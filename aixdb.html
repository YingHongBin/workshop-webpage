Abstract

摘要

When Data Meets AI: System, Learning, and Application is the theme of this year's workshop. Under this theme, the workshop is dedicated to exploring the latest research breakthroughs and innovative techniques at the fusion of artificial intelligence and database systems. In particular, our goal is to advance the development of next-generation data systems that can better meet end-users' demands for intelligent and autonomous data management and analytics.

当数据遇见人工智能：系统、学习与应用是今年研讨会的主题。在此主题下，研讨会致力于探索人工智能与数据库系统融合的最新研究突破和创新技术。特别是，我们的目标是推进下一代数据系统的发展，更好地满足最终用户对智能化和自主化数据管理与分析的需求。
